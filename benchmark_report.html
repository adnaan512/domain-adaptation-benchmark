Research Questions

RQ1 — Do TTA methods improve accuracy consistently across all corruptions?
Best method: TENT. Pseudo-label degrades on blur corruptions (confirmation bias). TTN and TENT improve consistently on noise and weather corruptions.

RQ2 — Does TENT outperform TTN across different severities?
Yes — TENT mCE (0.8823) < TTN mCE (0.8932): entropy minimisation outperforms stat-only adaptation.

RQ3 — Can pre-adaptation entropy predict which corruptions benefit most from TTA?
r = +0.4788. Moderate support.
